# PermitPro Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

PermitPro's GTM strategy follows a phased approach: (1) Establish product-market fit in Austin, TX, (2) Expand to top 20 metros, (3) Scale nationally. We leverage product-led growth for homeowners and high-touch sales for enterprise, with a target of $500K ARR in 12 months.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **US Building Permits Issued:** 1.8M annually (residential)
- **Average Permit Cost (fees + time):** $450
- **TAM:** $810M annually

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- **Top 100 US metros:** 1.2M permits annually
- **SAM:** $540M annually

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- **Year 1 Target:** 0.1% market share
- **SOM:** $540K (Year 1)
- **Year 3 Target:** 2.5% market share
- **SOM:** $13.5M (Year 3)

## Sales Strategy (Enterprise)

### Sales Process

**Stage 1: Prospecting**
- Source: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo
- Criteria: Construction companies, 50+ employees, multi-state
- Outreach: Email sequences, LinkedIn InMail
- Goal: Book discovery call

**Stage 2: Discovery (30 min call)**
- Qualify: BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Understand: Current permit process, pain points
- Demo: Brief product overview (10 min)
- Next step: Schedule full demo

**Stage 3: Demo (60 min call)**
- Custom demo using their real projects
- Show ROI calculator
- Address objections
- Next step: Trial or proposal

**Stage 4: Trial (14-30 days)**
- Onboard pilot team (5-10 users)
- Weekly check-ins
- Collect feedback and success metrics
- Next step: Expansion proposal

**Stage 5: Negotiation**
- Present proposal with ROI analysis
- Address procurement requirements
- Security/compliance review
- Legal review of contract

**Stage 6: Closed Won**
- Kickoff call with all stakeholders
- Implementation plan
- Training sessions
- Assign CSM

**Average Sales Cycle:** 90-180 days
**Win Rate Target:** 25%

## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks

**1. Slow Adoption**
*Mitigation:*
- Start in single market to prove model
- Iterate quickly based on feedback
- Generous free tier to reduce friction

**2. Competitor Entry**
*Mitigation:*
- Build data moat quickly
- Focus on superior UX
- Lock in partnerships

**3. Regulatory Pushback**
*Mitigation:*
- Position as assistance tool, not replacement
- Work with municipalities, not against them
- Offer hybrid (AI + human) option

**4. Scaling Challenges**
*Mitigation:*
- Hire experienced operators early
- Build scalable systems from day 1
- Raise adequate capital

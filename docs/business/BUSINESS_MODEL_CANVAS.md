# PermitPro Business Model Canvas

## Executive Summary

PermitPro disrupts the $50B+ building permit industry by using AI to automate permit discovery, form completion, and submission. We target homeowners and contractors frustrated with bureaucratic processes, offering a freemium SaaS model with per-permit pricing.

## Risk Analysis

### Key Risks & Mitigations

**1. Regulatory Risk**
*Risk:* Municipalities ban automated submissions
*Mitigation:* Position as assistance tool, maintain human review option, work with municipalities

**2. AI Accuracy Risk**
*Risk:* AI makes costly errors
*Mitigation:* Confidence scoring, human review for low-confidence, insurance policy

**3. Competitive Risk**
*Risk:* Large incumbents copy our model
*Mitigation:* Move fast, build moats (data, brand), focus on superior UX

**4. Data Quality Risk**
*Risk:* Scraped data becomes outdated
*Mitigation:* Automated monitoring, crowdsourced updates, direct municipal partnerships

**5. Economic Risk**
*Risk:* Construction slowdown reduces demand
*Mitigation:* Diversify into renovations (less cyclical), expand to commercial

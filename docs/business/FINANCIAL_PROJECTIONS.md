# PermitPro Financial Projections (2025-2027)

PermitPro is projecting aggressive growth from $659K ARR in Year 1 to $13.8M ARR in Year 3, achieving profitability in Month 24. We require $2M seed funding (secured) and plan to raise $10M Series A in Q3 2025.

## Sensitivity Analysis

### Revenue Scenarios

| Scenario | Year 3 ARR | Probability | Key Assumptions |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Best Case** | $30M | 20% | 300% YoY growth, 50% lower churn |
| **Base Case** | $22M | 60% | 250% YoY growth, 3% churn |
| **Conservative** | $15M | 20% | 150% YoY growth, 5% churn |

### Key Variables Impact

**10% increase in conversion rate (free→paid):**
- Revenue impact: +$2.2M ARR (Year 3)
- Profitability: 6 months earlier

**10% decrease in churn:**
- Revenue impact: +$3.5M ARR (Year 3)
- LTV increase: +30%

**20% increase in CAC:**
- Profitability delay: 4 months
- LTV/CAC: Still >3x (acceptable)
